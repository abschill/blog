About Me

My Name is Alex, I am from the United States, and I'm a minimalist full stack developer who enjoys helping people learn to code. I made this website with my own library, which you can find here. I do this full time, so I don't need or want your money, I have no aspirations of becoming an influencer so you don't have to worry about me trying to "market" you or something. I don't plan to change this, as I said I enjoy doing this, so engagement is pretty irrelevant to me as long as [the site] can help you, the individual reading this.

I have courses on things like the HTTP Protocol and the React UI Library, and the Amazon Web Services Infastructure if you are interested in full stack development. I also write articles where we explore various topics relating to the development industry or specific technologies. You can find those here.

My interests outside of my work include Boxing/BJJ, Wilderness/Survival, and Embedded Engineering.
